Given that I had to take charge and troubleshoot through countless technical difficulties that needed to be solved, my projects were challenging yet fulfilling research experiences.

I'm excited to work with awesome professor and team members.

As a PhD student, I hope to make a contribution to the development of security by combining my industry experiences I had with the knowledge of computer security. I firmly believe that my endeavor and passion in the field of security will contribute value to the global community, ultimately dedicating to the advancement of preventing cyber attack from hackers. And also, I hope to become a leading researcher, leading to the success of my future career.
